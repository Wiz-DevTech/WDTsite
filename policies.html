    Privacy Policy

    WizDevTech ("we," "our," "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

    Information We Collect

    Information you provide

    • Contact information (name, email, company) submitted via forms on this site
    • Project descriptions and business details submitted in execution request forms
    • Resume and application materials submitted via the careers form
    • Survey responses submitted via the site survey

    Information collected automatically

    • Site visit data (pages visited, time on page, referral source) via Google Analytics 4
    • Behavioral analytics via Mixpanel to understand feature usage
    • Standard server logs including IP address, browser type, and device type
    • Cookies as described in our Cookie Policy

    How We Use Your Information

    • To respond to execution requests, sales inquiries, and job applications
    • To contact you about potential engagements or follow-up questions
    • To improve our website and services based on usage patterns
    • To process your inquiry through our CRM (HubSpot) for lead management
    • To send transactional communications related to an engagement
    • To comply with legal obligations

    We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes.

    Information Sharing

    We share your information only with the following limited parties:

    • HubSpot — CRM platform used to manage leads and client communications
    • Google Analytics — Anonymized site analytics (no personally identifiable data shared)
    • Mixpanel — Product analytics for site behavior (pseudonymized)
    • Legal authorities — When required by law or valid legal process

    Your Rights

    Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

    • Access the personal information we hold about you
    • Request correction of inaccurate data
    • Request deletion of your personal information
    • Object to or restrict processing of your data
    • Request a portable copy of your data
    • Withdraw consent at any time where processing is consent-based

    Cookie Policy

    This Cookie Policy explains how WizDevTech uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website. By using our site, you consent to the use of cookies as described below.

    What Are Cookies

    Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow the site to recognize your browser and remember information about your visit. We use both session cookies (which expire when you close your browser) and persistent cookies (which remain for a set period).

    Cookies We Use

    Essential Cookies

    Required for the website to function properly. These cannot be disabled.

    • Session management cookies
    • Form security tokens
    • User preference storage (local, not transmitted)

    Analytics Cookies

    Help us understand how visitors use our site.

    • Google Analytics 4 — Tracks page views, session duration, and user behavior. IP addresses are anonymized. Google Privacy Policy →
    • Mixpanel — Tracks site interactions and feature usage. Data is pseudonymized. Mixpanel Privacy Policy →

    CRM Cookies

    Used to manage lead tracking and form submission data.

    • HubSpot — Tracks form submissions and page visits for lead management. HubSpot Privacy Policy →

    Managing Cookies

    You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

    • View what cookies are stored and delete them
    • Block third-party cookies
    • Block cookies from specific sites
    • Block all cookies (note: this may break some site functionality)
    • Clear all cookies when you close your browser

    For Google Analytics opt-out, you can use the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
