Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

WizDevTech respects your privacy and is committed to protecting personal information you share with us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and handle your data.

Information We Collect

  • Contact information submitted via our forms (name, email, company)
  • Project details and engagement descriptions you provide
  • Technical data including IP address, browser type, and pages visited
  • Communications you send to our email addresses

How We Use Your Information

  • To assess and respond to execution requests and inquiries
  • To deliver contracted services within active engagements
  • To communicate project status and deliverables
  • To improve our services based on aggregate usage patterns
  • To comply with legal obligations

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share data with service providers who assist in operating our website, subject to confidentiality agreements. We may disclose information if required by law.

Data Retention

Engagement records are retained for 3 years following engagement conclusion. Contact form submissions are retained for 12 months. You may request deletion at any time by contacting legal@wizdevtech.com.

Your Rights

  • Right to access your personal data we hold
  • Right to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to request deletion of your data
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

This site uses a minimal set of cookies. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking pixels.

Cookies We Use

  • Essential cookies: Required for basic site functionality. Cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand aggregate usage patterns. No personally identifiable information is collected.
  • Survey data: Stored in localStorage (not transmitted) to display aggregate survey results.

Third-Party

Google Fonts is loaded from Google's CDN, which may set performance cookies. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or other marketing trackers by default.

Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may affect site functionality.

Engagement Terms

Last updated: January 1, 2025

These terms supplement individual Scope of Work agreements and apply to all WizDevTech client engagements.

Scope & Deliverables

All deliverables are defined in writing prior to engagement commencement. Work outside the agreed scope is documented and presented as a separate engagement option.

Payment

  • 50% of total fee due at engagement kickoff
  • Remaining 50% due upon handoff delivery
  • Late payments accrue interest at 1.5% per month
  • Non-payment may result in work stoppage

Confidentiality

WizDevTech treats all client systems, data, and business information as strictly confidential. NDAs are available upon request prior to engagement.

Client Responsibilities

  • Provide timely access to necessary systems and documentation
  • Designate a single point of contact for the engagement
  • Respond to written requests within 1 business day
  • Review and approve or reject deliverables within 3 business days

Termination

Either party may terminate with 5 business days written notice. Client is responsible for payment for all work completed to the termination date.
