Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2024

WizDevTech respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us.

Information We Collect

  • Contact information submitted via our forms (name, email, company)
  • Project details and engagement descriptions you provide
  • Technical data including IP address and pages visited (via Supabase analytics)
  • Communications you send to our email addresses

How We Use Your Information

  • To assess and respond to execution requests and inquiries
  • To deliver contracted services within active engagements
  • To communicate project status and deliverables
  • To improve our services based on aggregate usage patterns
  • To comply with legal obligations

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share data with service providers who assist in delivering services, subject to confidentiality agreements.

Data Retention

Engagement records are retained for 3 years following conclusion. Contact form submissions are retained for 12 months. Request deletion at any time by contacting [email protected].

Your Rights

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to request deletion
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

Engagement Terms

Last updated: January 1, 2024

These terms supplement individual Scope of Work agreements and apply to all WizDevTech client engagements.

Scope & Deliverables

All deliverables are defined in writing prior to engagement commencement. Work outside agreed scope is documented and presented as a separate engagement option.

Payment

  • 50% of total fee due at kickoff
  • Remaining 50% due upon handoff delivery
  • Late payments accrue interest at 1.5% per month
  • Non-payment may result in work stoppage

Confidentiality

WizDevTech treats all client systems, data, and business information as strictly confidential. NDAs are available upon request prior to engagement.

Client Responsibilities

  • Provide timely access to necessary systems and documentation
  • Designate a single point of contact
  • Respond to written requests within 1 business day
  • Review deliverables within 3 business days of submission

Government Procurement

WizDevTech registers under NAICS 541511 as primary for SAM.gov purposes, with 541512 and 518210 as secondary codes. Under SBA size standards for NAICS 541511 (annual receipts threshold: $34M), WizDevTech qualifies as a small business for federal procurement purposes.

Excluded Classifications

WizDevTech does not operate under NAICS 541611 (Management Consulting) or 541613 (Marketing Consulting). These are deliberately excluded from our positioning. We execute against defined scope — we do not provide advisory, strategic, or consulting services.
