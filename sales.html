What You're Buying

Four execution services. All scoped. All delivered.

Every engagement ends with a documented handoff. These aren't open-ended commitments—they're defined work packages.

⚙

Finish Half-Built Systems

We complete tools, documentation, boards, and setups that were started but abandoned. Typical engagement 2–4 weeks. Ends with a working system and handoff documentation.

Repair Breaking Workflows

Convert Notes to Tasks
