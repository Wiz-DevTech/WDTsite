Market Position

Where we fit. What we’re not.

WizDevTech occupies the gap between traditional consulting and staff augmentation — a productized execution model with fixed scope, fixed deliverables, and a clean exit.

📈 Demand Drivers

This model thrives when organizations can’t justify full-time specialized hires but have real operational backlogs. Post-2022 headcount reductions left many teams with half-finished systems and no internal capacity to complete them.

📋 Productized Services

Unlike open-ended consulting, our engagements are repeatable and packaged — defined entry points, defined deliverables, defined exit. Shorter deal cycles and faster procurement decisions.

💳 Revenue Model

$5K–$100K+ engagement windows with 2–6 week timelines. Procurement typically flows through discretionary operational budgets rather than capital expenditure — reducing approval friction.

🎯 Competitive Posture

Sharp ICP deliberately trades volume for conversion quality. Firms who need strategy, discovery, or open-ended support are redirected — keeping the pipeline qualified and engagement success rates high.

✕ Not This

WizDevTech is not a management consulting firm (NAICS 541611) and is not a marketing consultancy (NAICS 541613). We do not advise, coach, or run open-ended engagements. We execute against defined scope and exit cleanly. If you need a strategic partner or discovery process before committing to scoped work, we’re not the right fit — and we’ll tell you that upfront.
