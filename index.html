04 / Leadership

The person accountable.

TM
Teri Massey
Founder & Executive Director

Teri built WizDevTech around a single observation: most organizations don’t fail from lack of strategy—they fail from lack of execution. With a background in operational systems and embedded delivery, she created an engagement model built entirely around completing defined work within defined windows. No retainers, no ambiguity, no scope creep.

Her work spans rescuing stalled software builds, repairing broken automation stacks, completing abandoned vendor implementations, and stabilizing data migrations for small businesses and scaling teams across the United States.

Systems & Problems We Solve

What we commonly fix.

We specialize in completing and stabilizing the following types of stalled or broken work. If your situation appears below, we can likely help.

Stalled Software Projects

Half-built apps, incomplete SaaS builds, and abandoned development projects that need a team to finish them on a fixed timeline.

Broken Automation Workflows

Failed or fragile automations in Zapier, Make, n8n, or custom scripts. We repair broken workflow logic, reconnect integrations, and document how everything works.

Data Migrations Gone Wrong

Incomplete or messy data migrations between CRMs, databases, spreadsheets, or platforms. We clean, structure, and complete the move with a documented handoff.

Abandoned Vendor Implementations

Implementations started by a vendor or contractor who disengaged. We pick up mid-stream and complete the defined scope.

Failing CI/CD Pipelines

Broken deployment pipelines, infrastructure drift, and unstable DevOps setups. We stabilize and document so your team can maintain it independently.

Notion, Airtable & Ops Stack Buildouts

Unfinished internal tools — Notion workspaces, Airtable bases, dashboards — started but never fully configured or handed off.
